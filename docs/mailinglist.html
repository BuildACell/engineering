[Build-a-cell] Workshop #4 and NSF Idea Machine submission

10/15/2018

Dear Build-a-Cell members and sympathizers;

The Build-a-Cell workshop #4 will be hosted by J Craig Venter Institute in February 8 2019.
http://buildacell.io/engineering/workshop4/
As before, the workshop will be 1 day long, with optional social activities the night before.

NSF 2026 Idea Machine
We will submit proposal for the NSF 2026 Idea Machine competition. The entire submitting team has to be US based, so we will send the entry from the Build-a-Cell steering team. We would, however, like the proposal to be representing thoughts of most of our community.
Here is the draft of the submission we are preparing:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KzXAb_tss6h3og85Zbz8OKHR7v5jVLmf8lDmV243aII/edit?usp=sharing Please give your comments and suggestions. Especially, please let us know what is missing. The deadline is October 26.

Best,
